Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: What is Stika and how does it work?
A: Stika is a tricycle advertising platform that connects agencies with tricycle riders for out-of-home advertising campaigns. Agencies create campaigns, set target locations (geofences), and riders display advertisements while operating in those areas.
Q: What types of users can access the platform?
A: Stika supports multiple user types:
  • Agency Users: Create and manage advertising campaigns
  • Clients: View-only access to campaigns created for them
  • Fleet Owners: Manage tricycle fleets and riders
  • Riders: Participate in advertising campaigns
Q: How do I get started as an agency?
A: 1. Sign up with your email and verify your account, 2. Complete your agency profile with business information, 3. Add funds to your wallet, 4. Create your first campaign with target locations and requirements.

Account & Profile Management

Q: How do I verify my agency account?
A: Complete your agency profile with business information including registration number, tax ID, address, and contact details. Upload required verification documents through your settings page.
Q: Can I invite clients to view their campaigns?
A: Yes! You can generate invite codes for clients or send them join requests. Clients get view-only access to campaigns created for them.
Q: How do I update my agency information?
A: Go to Settings > Profile to update your agency name, address, contact information, and business details.

Campaign Management

Q: What is a geofence and how do I set it up?
A: A geofence is a virtual boundary around a specific location where you want tricycles to display your advertisement. Use our map tool to draw areas around target locations like markets, schools, or business districts.
Q: How are campaign rates calculated?
A: Rates are suggested based on:
  • Location desirability and traffic volume
  • Campaign duration and time slots
  • Geofence size and complexity
  • Rider availability in the target area
Q: Can I pause or stop a campaign once it's started?
A: No, you cannot pause campaign once it's started, you can only stop it.
Q: How do I track campaign performance?
A: View real-time tracking on your dashboard including:
  • Number of active riders in geofences
  • Time spent in target areas
  • Location verification photos
  • Route coverage maps

Payments & Billing

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept bank transfers, card payments, and other Nigerian payment methods. All payments go to your agency wallet first, then campaigns are funded from there.
Q: How does the billing work?
A: Campaigns are pre-funded from your wallet balance. Billing occurs based on verified time riders spend in your geofenced areas displaying your advertisement.
Q: What happens if campaign geofence budget is exhausted before the end date?
A: The Campaign in the geofence automatically stops when it's budget is exhausted by the riders.
Q: What happens if campaign ends with budget leftover?
A: The difference between the set budget and the actual spent is returned to the funding wallet (client and agency), proportionately.

Fleet & Rider Management

Q: How do riders join campaigns?
A: Riders can signup through the Stika Rider app or Stika Fleet app. The fleet owner can register all riders (including those without android phones) through the Stika Fleet app.
Q: What verification is required for riders?
A: Riders must provide:
  • Valid tricycle registration
  • Driver's license/ID verification
  • Location verification photos
  • Fleet captain approval (for non-android phone users)
Q: I don't have an android phone, can I join any campaign geofence?
A: Riders without android phones can only join campaign geofences with fixed daily payment rates. The per hour geofences can only be joined by riders with android phones.
Q: How do you ensure riders are actually in the target areas?
A: Multiple verification methods:
  • GPS tracking with geofence monitoring
  • Random verification photo requests
  • Fleet captain oversight
  • Time-stamped location logs

Technical Issues

Q: Why can't I create a campaign?
A: Common reasons:
  • Incomplete agency profile
  • Insufficient wallet balance
  • Invalid geofence coordinates
  • Missing campaign requirements
Q: The map isn't loading properly. What should I do?
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing browser cache, or using a different browser. Contact support if the issue persists.
Q: How do I download campaign reports?
A: Go to your campaign dashboard and click the "Export" or "Download Report" button for detailed analytics and performance data.
