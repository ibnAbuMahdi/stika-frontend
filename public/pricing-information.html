How Stika Pricing Works

Stika operates on a simple, pay-per-campaign model with transparent fees. No monthly subscriptions, no hidden charges - just straightforward pricing that scales with your advertising needs.

Platform Fees

Campaign Creation Fee

5% of Campaign Budget

When you create a campaign, Stika charges a one-time platform fee of 5% of your total campaign budget.

  • Fee is charged at campaign creation
  • Non-refundable once campaign is launched
  • Covers platform operations, technology, and support
  • Same rate for all agencies and clients

Important Note

The 5% platform fee is only refundable if you cancel the campaign before it launches. Once a campaign is launched, the fee becomes non-refundable.

Campaign Rate Structure

Rate Types for Riders

As an agency, you set competitive rates for riders in each geofence. There are two rate types:

1. Per Hour Rate

  • Riders are paid for actual hours worked in the geofence
  • Requires Android phones with GPS tracking
  • Real-time location verification
  • Flexible for riders with varying schedules

2. Fixed Daily Rate

  • Riders receive a fixed payment per day
  • Suitable for riders without Android phones
  • Requires fleet captain verification
  • Additional ₦50 verification fee per verification

Verification Fees (Fixed Daily Rate Only)

₦50 per Verification

  • Applies only to fixed daily rate campaigns
  • Paid to fleet captains who perform verifications
  • Added to your total campaign cost
  • Ensures rider compliance without GPS tracking

Rider Earnings

Platform Fee for Riders

5% of Earnings

Riders see rates that are 5% less than what agencies set, as Stika deducts a 5% platform fee from rider earnings.

Example

  • Agency sets rate: ₦200/hour
  • Rider sees: ₦190/hour (after 5% deduction)
  • Platform fee: ₦10/hour

Payment Process

How It Works

  1. Fund Your Wallet: Add funds to your agency wallet via bank transfer or card
  2. Create Campaign: Set your budget and rates for each geofence
  3. Platform Fee Deduction: 5% is automatically deducted upon campaign creation
  4. Campaign Launch: Remaining budget is allocated for rider payments
  5. Real-time Tracking: Monitor spending as riders work
  6. Automatic Payments: Riders are paid based on verified work

Budget Management

Campaign Budget Controls

  • Pre-funded Model: Campaigns must be fully funded before launch
  • Real-time Monitoring: Track spending against budget in real-time
  • Automatic Stopping: Campaigns pause when budget is exhausted
  • Budget Refunds: Unused budget is returned to wallet after campaign ends

Shared Funding Options

Campaigns can be funded by:

  • Agency Only: Full budget from agency wallet
  • Client Only: Full budget from client wallet
  • Shared Funding: Split between agency and client wallets

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are there any hidden fees?

A: No. The only fees are the 5% platform fee on campaign creation and ₦50 verification fees for fixed daily rate campaigns.

Q: Can I get a refund on the platform fee?

A: The platform fee is only refundable if you cancel the campaign before launching it. Once launched, the fee is non-refundable.

Q: How do verification fees work?

A: For fixed daily rate campaigns, fleet captains verify rider participation. Each verification costs ₦50, paid to the fleet captain. This ensures compliance for riders without smartphones.

Q: What happens to unused campaign budget?

A: Any unused budget after a campaign ends is automatically returned to the funding wallet(s), proportionally if shared funding was used.
